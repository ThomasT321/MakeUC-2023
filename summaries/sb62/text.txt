A B I L L
To enact section 5.2316 of the Revised Code to
designate November 19th as "James A. Garfield
Day."
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO:
Section 1. That section 5.2316 of the Revised Code be
enacted to read as follows:
Sec. 5.2316. The nineteenth day of November is designated
as "James A. Garfield Day."
